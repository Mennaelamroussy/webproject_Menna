About Us

Welcome to our online kitchen! We founded this business in 2002 with the help of passed down recipes from our beloved friends and family :) It all started when we met in a corporate company in Richmond, Virginia, we fell in love and 1 year later we got married. After, we realized the corporate life was not for us so we both daringly quit our jobs and thus the birth of Doughy Delights.


Our recipes are classic and approachable, inspired by our favorite seasonal flavors and recipes passed down in our families. We also enjoy writing informational posts about different baking techniques and how to use proper ingredients to achieve the best results. We want everyone to learn, grow, and have confidence in the kitchen. Our goal is to help readers feel like a master in the kitchen! And, more importantly, we want everyone to enjoy the experience of making really really good desserts. In butter and chocolate, we unite!
